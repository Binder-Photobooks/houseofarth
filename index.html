About Us

Crafting Stories
on Paper

For over two decades, House of Arth has been New Delhi's most trusted name in premium stationery and commercial print. Nestled in Naraina Industrial Area, our facility brings together cutting-edge machinery and artisan expertise under one roof.

We believe every printed piece is a reflection of your brand's soul. From delicate letterhead to bold packaging, we bring precision, passion, and an unrelenting eye for detail to everything we do.

Everything You Need,
One Address

From ideation to dispatch — our end-to-end print ecosystem means no middlemen, no delays, and no compromise on quality.

01

Design Studio

In-house creative team to conceptualise, typeset, and prepare artwork for any print format — from business stationery to mailers.

02

Pre-Press & Proofing

Colour-calibrated proofing, trapping, and imposition done in-house — your print runs start right, every time.

03

Digital Printing

HP Indigo and high-speed digital for short-run, variable data, and rush print jobs with zero setup charges.

04

Offset Printing

4- and 5-colour offset presses for long-run jobs where consistent ink density and Pantone accuracy are paramount.

05

Finishing

UV, aqueous lamination, hot foil, embossing, die-cutting, and perfect binding — all done on-site for faster turnaround.

06

Packaging & Dispatch

Secure packing with PAN-India courier delivery. Bulk orders managed with custom scheduling and tracking.
